##  Project Overview

The **Road Accident Dashboard** is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze road accident data, casualty trends, vehicle involvement, road conditions, and accident severity. This dashboard provides valuable insights for transportation authorities, policymakers, and safety analysts to identify risk factors and improve road safety measures.

---

##  Project Objectives

- Monitor total road accident casualties.
- Analyze accident severity levels.
- Identify vehicle types involved in accidents.
- Track monthly casualty trends.
- Evaluate accident distribution by road type and surface condition.
- Analyze casualties based on location and lighting conditions.
- Enable dynamic filtering by year and area.

##  Dashboard Insights

### Accident Severity Analysis
- Slight casualties account for the majority of accident cases.
- Serious casualties represent a significant proportion of total accidents.
- Fatal casualties contribute a relatively small percentage but remain critical for safety analysis.

### Vehicle Type Analysis
The dashboard tracks casualties involving:
- Cars
- Vans
- Buses
- Bicycles
- Agricultural Vehicles
- Other Vehicle Types

### Monthly Casualty Trend
- Compares Current Year (CY) and Previous Year (PY) casualty trends.
- Helps identify seasonal accident patterns.
- Supports year-over-year performance analysis.

### Road Type Analysis
Accidents are categorized by:
- Single Carriageway
- Dual Carriageway
- Roundabout
- One-Way Street
- Slip Road

### Road Surface Analysis
Accidents are analyzed based on:
- Dry Surface
- Wet Surface
- Snow/Ice Conditions

### Location Analysis
- Urban Areas
- Rural Areas

### Light Condition Analysis
- Daylight
- Darkness
